## 1. PURPOSE

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship relating to municipal digital services, payment infrastructure, and citizen engagement technology ("the Purpose"), and in connection with the Purpose, each Party may disclose to the other certain confidential and proprietary information.

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by either Party to the other, whether oral, written, electronic, or in any other form, including but not limited to: business plans, technical architecture, source code, integration methods, pricing models, financial information, customer or municipal relationships, pilot data, and any other information that is identified as confidential or that would reasonably be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

## 3. OBLIGATIONS

Each Party receiving Confidential Information ("Receiving Party") agrees to:

1. hold the Confidential Information in strict confidence;
2. not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing Party ("Disclosing Party");
3. use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;
4. not use the Confidential Information to develop, design, or build any competing product, service, or business;
5. take reasonable steps to protect the confidentiality of the information, at least equivalent to the steps it takes to protect its own confidential information.

## 4. EXCLUSIONS

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

1. is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
2. was already lawfully known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure;
3. is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of the Confidential Information;
4. is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order, provided the Receiving Party gives the Disclosing Party prompt notice where legally permitted.

## 5. NO LICENSE OR OWNERSHIP TRANSFER

Nothing in this Agreement grants either Party any right, title, license, or ownership interest in the other Party's Confidential Information, intellectual property, or business concepts. All Confidential Information remains the property of the Disclosing Party.

## 6. TERM

This Agreement remains in effect for a period of three (3) years from the date of signature, regardless of whether the Parties proceed with the Purpose.

## 7. REMEDIES

The Parties acknowledge that a breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages alone would be an inadequate remedy, and the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedies available at law.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa, and the Parties submit to the jurisdiction of the South African courts.

## 9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties relating to confidentiality for the Purpose and supersedes all prior discussions or agreements relating to the same subject.
