## 1. BACKGROUND

1.1 The Contributor will provide services to the Company, which may include software development, design, business development, or other contributions, in connection with the Company's municipal digital services and payment infrastructure platform ("the Platform").

1.2 The Parties wish to record the terms on which any intellectual property created by the Contributor in connection with the Platform shall belong to the Company, and the confidentiality obligations applicable to the Contributor.

## 2. ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

2.1 The Contributor hereby assigns to the Company, absolutely and irrevocably, all right, title, and interest, including all intellectual property rights (copyright, database rights, design rights, patent rights, and any other proprietary rights, whether registered or unregistered, anywhere in the world) in any work, code, design, documentation, idea, or other material created, conceived, or developed by the Contributor in connection with the Platform, whether alone or jointly with others, during the period of their engagement with the Company ("Work Product").

2.2 The Contributor agrees to promptly disclose all Work Product to the Company and to execute any further documents reasonably required by the Company to perfect this assignment.

2.3 To the extent any Work Product cannot be assigned under applicable law, the Contributor grants the Company an exclusive, perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, modify, and distribute such Work Product for any purpose.

2.4 The Contributor waives any moral rights in the Work Product to the fullest extent permitted by law.

## 3. PRE-EXISTING IP

3.1 Any intellectual property owned by the Contributor prior to this Agreement, and not created in connection with the Platform, is listed in Annexure A and is excluded from the assignment in clause 2.

3.2 If no items are listed in Annexure A, the Contributor warrants that they have no pre-existing intellectual property relevant to the Platform.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY

4.1 The Contributor acknowledges that in the course of their engagement they will have access to confidential information of the Company, including business plans, technical architecture, integration methods, pricing models, and municipal relationships ("Confidential Information").

4.2 The Contributor agrees to keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential, both during and after their engagement with the Company, and not to disclose it to any third party or use it for any purpose other than the performance of their duties to the Company.

## 5. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

5.1 For a period of twelve (12) months following the termination of the Contributor's engagement with the Company, the Contributor agrees not to, directly or indirectly:

   (a) develop, build, or assist in developing or building any product or service that competes with the Platform in the South African municipal digital services or payment infrastructure market;

   (b) solicit or attempt to solicit any municipality, client, or contact introduced to the Contributor through their engagement with the Company, for the purpose of providing competing services;

   (c) solicit any other employee, contractor, or founder of the Company to leave their engagement with the Company.

5.2 The Parties agree that this restraint is reasonable in scope, duration, and geography given the nature of the Company's business, and is necessary to protect the Company's legitimate proprietary interests.

7.1 Unless otherwise agreed in a separate written employment agreement, this Agreement does not create an employment relationship between the Contributor and the Company.
